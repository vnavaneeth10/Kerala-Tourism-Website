Kasargod

The northernmost district of Kerala, Kasaragod is famous for its handlooms and its 293 kilometre long coastline.Rich in history, Kasaragod is home to the largest and best preserved fort in the State - Bekal

Bekal Fort

Bekal Fort was built by Shivappa Nayaka of Keladi in 1650 AD, at Bekal. It is the largest fort in Kerala, spreading over 40 acres.

Ranipuram

Ranipuram (also known by its former name Madathumala) is a village and a major tourist attraction in the Kasaragod district of the Indian state of Kerala.
