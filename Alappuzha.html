Alappuzha

A town with canals, backwaters, beaches, and lagoons,called as the "Venice of the East." Hence, it is known as the "Venetian Capital" of Kerala

Alappuzha Beach

Also known as Alleppey Beach. The scenic beach has a 150-year old pier merging into the sea.

Vembanad Lake

The longest lake in India, Vembanad Lake flows across several districts in Kerala.
